# Contributing to the Reservoir

Thank you for considering a contribution to the Esoteric Reservoir.

This is a personal collection of antifragile systems at the intersection of code, sound, waves, hardware, and law. Contributions are welcome when they increase clarity, capability, or resilience.

## How to Contribute

1. **Start with an issue** — Use the existing templates (Bug Report or Feature/Idea Request).  
2. **Keep changes small and focused** — One idea per pull request.  
3. **Discuss first for larger work** — Open an issue before big refactors or new features so we stay aligned.

## What is welcome
- Bug fixes and performance improvements  
- New tools or scripts that match the existing minimalist style  
- Enhancements to music organization, analysis, or sonic experiments  
- Acoustic / wave propagation models and research  
- Documentation or automation improvements  
- Ideas that survive first-principles scrutiny

## What is not
- Large refactors without prior discussion  
- Feature bloat (“wouldn’t it be cool if…”)  
- Changes that reduce simplicity or introduce fragility

## Development notes
- Most Python work targets 3.10+ with minimal dependencies.  
- Music and creative contributions (compositions, analysis methods, etc.) are especially valued — describe the concept and its intended effect.

## Legal note
All repositories follow their individual licenses. By submitting a pull request you agree to license your contribution under the same terms.
